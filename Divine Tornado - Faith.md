## ⚔️ Build: Divine Tornado Call
https://youtu.be/S-soud0LfAQ?is=di53YZAGB0grr1OX

Driven by the howling winds of the Wild Hunt, Wotan crossed the fog into the Lands Between. He seeks to usurp the Golden Order, viewing the Greater Will as an artificial shackle upon the primal, chaotic currents of the sky. By seizing the Elden Ring, he intends to restore the true, unbridled reign of the storm.

* **Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral — he respects only the law of the gale and the hunt.
* **Motto:** "The storm does not negotiate; it harvests."

- Ash of War: Horn Calling (Horned Warrior's Sword, inherent), Storm Blade (Sacred Misericorde, inherent)

## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build optimises high Faith and Dexterity to create a devastating combination using the Divine Beast Tornado incantation alongside the Horned Warrior's Sword. The core strategy revolves around trapping opponents within the massive, multi-hitting area of effect of the Divine Beast Tornado, then immediately rolling forward or pursuing with the Horn Calling skill to true-combo or roll-catch them as they attempt to escape the vortex. Elden Stars can be used beforehand to create immense pressure, forcing opponents to roll constantly and making them highly vulnerable to being caught by the tornado. For long-range pressure, Lightning Spear and Giant Golden Arc offer excellent roll-catching tools, while the Roar of Rugalea serves as a powerful close-range surprise option to blast hyper-aggressive players away. Use the Buckler for reactive parries when facing predictable melee rushdowns, and switch to the Sacred Misericorde for massive critical punishes.

# ⚔️ Build: Divine Tornado Call — Complete Route Guide

Optimal progression route for assembling the Divine Tornado Call build, split between the Base Game and the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC.

---

### 🧭 Early to Mid Game Setup (Base Game)

* **Prophet Starting Class** 📜: Best starting attributes for immediate Faith priority and basic incantation tools.
* **Misericorde** 🗡️: Loot from a chest behind a Stonesword Key fog wall in Stormveil Castle.
* **Ash of War: Storm Blade** 💨: Purchase from Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack in Limgrave. Apply this to the Misericorde. Alter it to the Sacred affinity once you obtain the Sanctified Whetblade in Leyndell.
* **Buckler** 🛡️: Purchase from Gatekeeper Gostoc at the Stormveil Castle gates for optimal parry frames.
* **Radagon Icon** 💍: Found in a treasure chest on the second floor balcony of the Debate Parlor in Raya Lucaria Academy. Decreases spell casting time.
* **Lightning Spear** ⚡: Obtain the Dragon Cult Prayerbook by defeating the Leyndell Knight patrolling south of the Artist's Shack in Liurnia. Hand it to Corhyn or Miriel to unlock the incantation.

---

### 🏛️ Late Game Setup (Base Game)

* **Two Fingers Heirloom** 💍: Located in a chest within the underground cellar of the Purified Ruins in eastern Liurnia. Provides a flat +5 boost to Faith.
* **Golden Vow** ✨: Found on a corpse inside the Corpse-Stench Shack in western Mt. Gelmir.
* **Elden Stars** 🌟: Found in Deeproot Depths. Follow the massive roots extending from the Great Waterfall Crest site of grace into a small cave filled with ants.
* **Shard of Alexander** 💍: Complete Iron Fist Alexander’s questline. Defeat him in your final duel at Crumbling Farum Azula to claim this talisman.

---

### 🏔️ Shadow of the Erdtree Progression (DLC)

* **Beast Claw** 🐾: Defeat Logur, the Beast Claw hunter, who roams the wooded area in the southeastern region of Gravesite Plain.
* **Giant Golden Arc** ☀️: Travel to the Ruins of Unte via the stone coffin transport from Shadow Keep. Loot the incantation from a chest inside the ruins.
* **Roar of Rugalea** 🐻: Defeat the Great Red Bear boss, Rugalea, located in the northern forests of the Rauh Base region.
* **Horned Warrior's Sword & Armor Pieces** ⚔️: Farm the Greatsword-wielding Horned Warriors located throughout Belurat, Tower Settlement and Enir-Ilim. The Horned Warrior's Sword, Horned Warrior Gauntlets, and Horned Warrior Greaves drop from these enemies.
* **Enraged Divine Beast** 💍: Defeat the Divine Beast Dancing Lion boss in Belurat, Tower Settlement. Trade the Remembrance of the Dancing Lion with Enia at the Roundtable Hold.
* **Divine Beast Helm** 🦁: Guaranteed drop from defeating the Divine Beast Dancing Lion in Belurat, Tower Settlement.
* **Divine Beast Warrior Armor** 🦅: Farm the elite Divine Beast Warriors found in the upper sections of Enir-Ilim.
* **Divine Beast Tornado** 🌪️: Defeat the second Divine Beast Dancing Lion boss located in the Ancient Ruins of Rauh (accessible via the grand elevator platform past the bridge).
