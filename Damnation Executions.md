## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build optimises the raw physical damage and critical grab capabilities of the Greatsword of Damnation. The primary objective is to land the unique Ash of War, Golden Crux, which acts as a critical grab animation that can catch opponents out of standard dodge rolls. 

To execute the build effectively, use the complete Rakshasa armour set to gain an 8% total damage amplification across all attacks. Two-hand the weapon to activate the Two-Handed Sword Talisman bonus, which significantly enhances the standard Claymore-style move set, specifically the crouch poke and the thrusting heavy attack. Use the Shard of Alexander and the Dagger Talisman together to drastically boost the damage output of the Golden Crux grab, as the game scales its secondary animation phase as a critical strike. Deploy the thrusting heavy attacks to bait opponents into panic rolls, then immediately follow up with the Ash of War to lock them into the high-damage grab sequence. Use basic health regeneration incantations to counter the increased damage negation penalty caused by the Rakshasa armour.
