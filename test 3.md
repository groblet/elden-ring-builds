- Ash of War: Golden Parry (Standard)

## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build focuses purely on high Faith damage output using unblockable holy armaments and high-tier incantations for player versus player invasions. You can utilise the pure holy scaling of dual Coded Swords or Cipher Pata to bypass shields entirely. Keep pressure up from a distance using tracking incantations like Elden Stars and wide area-of-effect spells like Multilayered Ring of Light. When opponents try to close the gap, cast Wrath of Gold or Light of Miquella to punish aggressive groups or use Golden Parry to secure critical standard ripostes.
