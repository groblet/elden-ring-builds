## ⚔️ Build: Captain America

## 📊 Attributes
- Vigor: High (Prioritise for survival)
- Mind: Moderate (Sufficient for Ash of War usage)
- Endurance: High (Crucial for blocking and guard countering)
- Strength: High (Primary damage scaling for heavy affinity weapons)
- Dexterity: Minimum requirements
- Intelligence: Minimum requirements for shield usage
- Faith: Minimum requirements for shield usage
- Arcane: Base level

## 🗡️ Equipment
- Right Hand: Dane's Footwork (Heavy affinity), Dryleaf Arts (Heavy affinity)
- Left Hand: Smithscript Shield
- Armour: Leather Crown, [Chest Piece of choice], Noble's Gloves, Prisoner Trousers

## 💍 Talismans
- Shard of Alexander
- Curved Sword Talisman
- Shattered Stone Talisman
- Erdtree's Favour +2 or Smithing Talisman

## ✨ Spells & Ash of War
- Sorceries/Incantations: None required
- Ash of War: Heavy affinity applied to fist weapons
- Physic Tear: Deflecting Hardtear (essential for guard counter mechanics)

## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
The build relies on aggressive guard counters and mixed-range pressure. Use the Smithscript Shield to pelt opponents from a distance with shield throws and to block incoming attacks. Upon successfully blocking with the Deflecting Hardtear active, immediately perform a guard counter to punish the opponent. The fist weapons, Dane's Footwork and Dryleaf Arts, provide high-damage combos for close-quarters engagements. Manage your stamina carefully, as blocking and countering consume significant resources. Switch between the shield for zoning and the fist weapons for high-pressure punishes to keep opponents off balance.
