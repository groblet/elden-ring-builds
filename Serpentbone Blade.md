## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
 * Consume the Flask of Wondrous Physick containing the Blood-Sucking Cracked Tear and Thorny Cracked Tear before engaging.
 * Cast Golden Vow followed by either Flame, Grant Me Strength or a crafted Bloodboil Aromatic to significantly ramp up physical damage.
 * Trigger your attack buffs immediately at the start of a fight by inflicting self-poison using Rope Fetid Pots or the Deadly Poison Perfume Bottle, then quickly cure it with a Neutralizing Bolus.
 * This interaction triggers the passive attack power increases from both the Mushroom Crown and the Poison Hand simultaneously without needing to land a hit.
 * Aggressively chain the Double Slash weapon skill to stack rapid successive hits, maxing out the damage scaling from your talismans whilst letting the unique heavy attacks build up deadly poison on the target.
