## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build focuses on maximising the damage of rarely utilised magma sorceries. Lusat's Glintstone Staff is held in the main hand to provide the highest possible sorcery scaling, whilst the Gelmir Glintstone Staff is held in the off-hand to passively boost lava spell potency. The Talisman of Dread combined with both Graven talismans drastically elevates fire damage. 

To execute the build effectively, use Roiling Magma to set delayed explosive traps, then cast Collapsing Stars to pull opponents directly into the blast radius. Charged Magma Shot serves as the primary single-target projectile for heavy damage and field zoning, boosted by the Godfrey Icon. For aggressive groups or tight corridors, trigger Gelmir's Fury to unleash devastating area-of-effect lava fountains at close range. Mixed physics flask should utilise the Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear to further augment all fire output.
