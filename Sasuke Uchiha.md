## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build replicates Sasuke Uchiha's abilities by combining swift katana strikes with potent incantations. Equip the Dragonscale Blade to mimic the Chidori sword effect, or apply Black Flame Blade to the Uchigatana to simulate Amaterasu flame control. Rely on high dexterity to maximise casting speed, allowing you to weave spells like Giantsflame Take Thee and Honed Bolt seamlessly into close quarters combat. Use a fist weapon equipped with Lifesteal Fist to execute a devastating Chidori style grab attack. Maintain high aggression, manage spacing carefully, and utilise light rolls to evade enemy attacks while sustaining continuous elemental pressure.
