## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build focuses entirely on maximising the damage output of the Aspect of the Crucible: Wings Ash of War applied to a Heavy Greatsword. Consume a Bloodboil Aromatic and an Uplifting Aromatic before engagement to drastically elevate your attack rating. Use the skill to fly through opposing attacks, using the exceptional hyper armour to trade directly with the enemy. The Spear Talisman rewards these trades with massive pierce counter damage, frequently resulting in a single-hit elimination. The skill features a long lingering hitbox and immense forward momentum, making it an excellent counter against casting builds. If an opponent manages to evade the initial thrust, immediately trigger the heavy attack follow-up to catch them at the end of their roll.
