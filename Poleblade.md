# ⚔️ Build: Eleonora's Poleblade Destructive Bleed

## 📊 Attributes
- Vigor: Minimal investment for this no-hit variant, though points can be reallocated to target level 200
- Mind: Base stats or as needed for auxiliary buffs
- Endurance: Base stats or as needed to sustain the weapon skill
- Strength: Base requirements for equipment
- Dexterity: 60
- Intelligence: Base
- Faith: 33
- Arcane: 75

## 🗡️ Equipment
- Right Hand: Eleonora's Poleblade +10, Dagger with Assassin's Gambit
- Left Hand: Dagger with Raptor of the Mists, Any available Sacred Seal
- Armour: Rakshasa Armour Set (three pieces combined with White Mask), or Dancer Set for pure skill damage, or Iron Kasa with Ronin's Armour, White Reed Gauntlets, and White Reed Greaves

## 💍 Talismans
- Shard of Alexander
- Lord of Blood's Exultation
- Millicent's Prosthesis
- Rotten Winged Sword Insignia

## ✨ Spells & Ash of War
- Sorceries/Incantations: Golden Vow, Howl of Shabriri, Swarm of Flies
- Ash of War: Bloodblade Dance (Unique to Eleonora's Poleblade), Raptor of the Mists (Standard Affinity on Dagger), Assassin's Gambit (Standard Affinity on Dagger)

## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build optimises Eleonora's Poleblade to achieve high bleed output and substantial stance damage. Prior to combat, apply Golden Vow followed by Howl of Shabriri to amplify overall damage. Swarm of Flies can be cast from a distance to rapidly build up status bars against resilient targets. In combat, rely heavily on the unique R2 heavy attacks and the Bloodblade Dance weapon skill to trigger consecutive hit buffs from the talismans whilst proccing blood loss. Use the off-hand daggers exclusively for utility, employing Assassin's Gambit for stealth or Raptor of the Mists to bypass complex boss hitboxes. Utilise the Blood-Sucking Cracked Tear and Thorny Cracked Tear in the Flask of Wondrous Physick to further stack consecutive attack power and raw damage output.
