# 🧙 Build Overview
The Erdsteel Dagger Faith Build focuses on maximizing consecutive attack damage by utilising the weapon's unique scaling, where both physical and elemental damage increase directly from the Faith stat. This playstyle relies on aggressive, fast-paced exchanges and heavy attacks to maintain constant pressure while two-handing the dagger.

# 📊 Stats
- **Level:** 125 (Pure Faith focus)
- **Vigor:** Sufficient for survival
- **Mind:** Sufficient for weapon arts
- **Endurance:** Sufficient for poise/stamina
- **Strength:** Base requirement levels
- **Dexterity:** Base requirement levels
- **Intelligence:** Base
- **Faith:** Primary scaling stat
- **Arcane:** Base

# ⚔️ Weapons
- **Erdsteel Dagger:** Infused with Sacred or Flame Art for optimal Faith scaling.
- **Talismans:**
  - **Rotten Winged Sword Insignia:** Increases attack power with successive attacks.
  - **Millicent's Prosthesis:** Boosts dexterity and raises attack power with successive attacks.
  - **Two-Handed Sword Talisman:** Increases damage output by 10% while two-handing.
  - **Bull-Goat's Talisman:** Increases poise to prevent interruption during close-range dagger attacks.

# 🧪 Buffs
- **Flame Art Setup:** Use Flame, Grant Me Strength alongside Flaming Strike for a significant damage boost.
- **Sacred Setup:** Use Golden Vow alongside the Sacred Blade ash of war for sustained damage increases.

# 🎯 Tactics
- **Aggressive Multi-Hit:** Prioritise the multi-hit heavy attack, which offers excellent forward momentum and poise damage, to exploit the dagger's high attack speed.
- **Two-Handing:** Maintain a two-handed grip on the dagger at all times to keep the damage bonus from the Two-Handed Sword Talisman active.
- **Positioning:** Because the dagger requires close-range combat, use the poise from the Bull-Goat's Talisman to avoid being stunned during trades.
- **Damage Stacking:** Use weapon arts like Flaming Strike or Sacred Blade as both a closing tool and an immediate weapon buff during combat.
- **Punishing:** Use the dagger’s speed to punish heal attempts and space opponents effectively, keeping up constant pressure to trigger the consecutive attack damage bonuses from your talismans.
