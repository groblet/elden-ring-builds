## 💍 Talismans

* **Blue Dancer Charm:** Grants 12% to 13% more physical damage due to the zero-weight gear setup.

* **Roar Medallion:** Increases the damage of breath-type spells.
* **Lord of Blood's Exultation:** Boosts attack power when blood loss is triggered nearby.

## ✨ Sorceries & Incantations

* **Dragonmaw:** Primary physical damage spell.
* **Greyoll's Roar:** Used for its debuff.
* **Dragonice:** Used to apply the frost debuff.
* **Rotten Breath:** Applies scarlet rot and inflicts physical damage.
* **Fire Serpent:** Cheap, fast projectile with tracking.
* **Golden Vow:** Buff.
* **Flame, Grant Me Strength:** Buff.
* **Blessing's Boon:** Passive health regeneration.

## 🧴 Buffs & Consumables

1. **Rock Heart:** Used to trigger the dragon transformation.
2. **Golden Vow**
3. **Flame, Grant Me Strength**
4. **Flask of Wondrous Physick:** Contains the Faith Tear (to meet spell requirements) and Opaline Tear (15% flat damage negation to offset being naked).
5. **Uchigatana (Blood Blade):** Spammed at point-blank range at the start of combat to trigger immediate blood loss, activating the Lord of Blood's Exultation buff.

## 📝 Notes

* **Infusion Mechanics:** Running an Occult infusion allows the Uchigatana and Blood Blade to scale entirely off Arcane, matching the scaling of the Dragon Communion Seals.
* **Physical Synergy:** Because the character is naked under the Rock Heart transformation, the Blue Dancer Charm heavily boosts physical damage. This directly amplifies the physical damage dealt by Dragonmaw and Rotten Breath, alongside the katana attacks.
* **Debuff Stacking:** Pre-buffing is designed to layer multipliers. Greyoll's Roar forces enemies to take 10% more damage, whilst the frost proc from Dragonice forces an additional 20% damage.
* **Health Management:** Blessing's Boon is cast specifically to offset the health-draining penalty of using the Blood Blade skill.

## 🗺️ Phase 1: Limgrave & Caelid (Early Game Base)

* **Uchigatana:** Pick up inside Deathtouched Catacombs (Stormhill, northern Limgrave). It rests on a corpse on a ledge in the skeleton-filled halls.
* **Blue Dancer Charm:** Defeat the Guardian Golem boss at the end of Highroad Cave, located at the northernmost tip of the Limgrave river canyon.
* **Roar Medallion:** Defeat the Stonedigger Troll boss at the bottom of the Limgrave Tunnels (northern tip of Agheel Lake).
* **Dragon Communion Seal & Early Spells:** Head to the Church of Dragon Communion (island off western Limgrave coast) or Cathedral of Dragon Communion (South Caelid). Purchase **Dragonice** and **Rotten Breath** using Dragon Hearts.

## 🌋 Phase 2: Liurnia, Altus Plateau & Mt. Gelmir

* **Flame, Grant Me Strength:** Located behind Fort Gael in Western Caelid, guarded by two Flame Chariots.
* **Golden Vow:** Found inside the Corpse-Stench Shack on the road leading up to Mt. Gelmir, northwest of the Altus Plateau.
* **Blood Blade (Ash of War):** Travel to the Altus Plateau. Locate the shallow pond northeast of the Erdtree-Gazing Hill Site of Grace. Kill the invisible Teardrop Scarab circling the water.
* **Blessing's Boon:** Purchase from Miriel, Pastor of Vows at the Church of Vows in Eastern Liurnia for 4,000 Runes.
* **Fire Serpent:** Found inside the Shadow Keep (Storehouse area) once you transition into the expansion.

## 🏛️ Phase 3: Endgame Base Game

* **Lord of Blood's Exultation:** Defeat Esgar, Priest of Blood inside the Leyndell Catacombs, accessed via the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds.
* **Shard of Alexander:** Complete Iron Fist Alexander's questline. The final duel occurs at Crumbling Farum Azula; defeating him yields the talisman.
* **Dragonmaw & Greyoll's Roar:** Defeat Great Wyrm Theodorix (Consecrated Snowfield) and Elder Dragon Greyoll (Caelid) to unlock these specific high-tier incantations at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion.

## 🏔️ Phase 4: Realm of Shadow (DLC Expansion)

* **Domain of Dragons Painting:** Head to the Shadow Keep Main Gate Plaza Site of Grace. Exit right, take the ramp up the bridge, then take another right along the wall to find a hidden ladder down. Follow the lower path through a waterfall into a room containing the painting.
* **Rock Heart:** Ascend the Jagged Peak mountain until you reach the Jagged Peak Mountainside Site of Grace. Head west past the spirit spring plume onto a stone bridge. At the edge sits a ghost on a chair; strike it to reveal the Rock Heart.
