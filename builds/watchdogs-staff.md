## 💍 Talismans

* **Ritual Sword Talisman:** Raises attack power when HP is at maximum.
* **Shard of Alexander:** Greatly boosts the attack power of skills.
* **Two-Handed Sword Talisman:** Enhances attacks with two-handed weapons.
* **Magic Scorpion Charm:** Raises magic attack, but lowers damage negation.

## 📝 Notes

* **Combat Loop:** Channel the Ash of War to spawn delayed magic projectiles, then immediately rush the enemy. Use your basic colossal weapon attacks to apply pressure and true combo into the projectiles (or vice versa).
* **Hyper Armour:** The skill possesses impressive hyper armour. You can poise through an attack while casting it to land a true combo, provided you have the HP to survive the trade.

Always verify the build before spending larval tears or upgrade materials. 🐛
