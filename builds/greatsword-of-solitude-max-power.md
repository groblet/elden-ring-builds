## Weapon: Greatsword of Solitude
- **Type:** Greatsword (Standard/Pierce)
- **Required Attributes:** 27 Str / 13 Dex
- **Scaling:** Str B (improves toward A/near-S at +10, ~171 scaling)
- **Ash of War:** Solitary Moon Slash (12 FP one-handed / 10 FP two-handed) — built-in, cannot be swapped or buffed
- **Weight:** 15.5
- **Guard:** 89% physical negation, 40% elemental negation, 53 guard boost (58.3 at +10)
- **Location:** Drop from the Blackgaol Knight boss, Western Nameless Mausoleum (Shadow of the Erdtree)
- **Notes:** Cannot be infused with Ashes of War or buffed with magic/consumables
- At 80 Str / 13 Dex: ~759 AR one-handed, ~816 AR two-handed

## Talismans
- **Shard of Alexander** — boosts the damage of the Ash of War's initial projectile/charge phase
- **Two-Handed Sword Talisman** — increases follow-up light attack damage when two-handing the weapon
- **Greatshield Talisman** (situational/defensive slot) — boosts guard absorption when blocking
- **Deflecting Hardtear** (Flask of Wondrous Physick) — punishes parries/guard counters for defensive utility

## Strategy / Playstyle
- Two-hand the Greatsword for max AR (~816) and to trigger the Two-Handed Sword Talisman bonus.
- **Solitary Moon Slash** is a burst-damage Ash of War: initial slash/projectile phase (boosted by Shard of Alexander), followed by a heavy delayed swing.
- The follow-up heavy swing doubles as a **delayed roll-catch** and **spacing tool** — its shoulder check can stun enemies, setting up the subsequent swing for guaranteed damage.
- Faith investment (30) likely supports incantations/buffs for sustain or utility (verify against video specifics if available).
- High Vigor (71) and Endurance (50) provide survivability and stamina to fully commit to the heavy two-hit combo.
