🧙 Build Overview
🩸 The Bloody Helice build is an Arcane-focused setup designed around heavy thrusting swords and rapid blood loss accumulation. The playstyle relies on exploiting the weapon skill's invincibility frames to evade enemy attacks and deliver high-damage counter-thrusts.
⚔️ Weapons
🗡️ Bloody Helice (+10): The primary heavy thrusting sword, which scales with Arcane to provide high physical damage and rapid bleed build-up.
📜 Erdtree Seal: Equipped solely to cast essential incantation buffs.
🦅 Red-Feathered Branchsword: The primary talisman used to maximise damage output when operating at low health.
🩰 Blue Dancer Charm: Utilised during the early stages of the playthrough when equipment load remains low.
🪓 Axe Talisman: Enhances the damage of fully charged thrusting attacks.
⚔️ Two-Handed Sword Talisman: Used as a temporary booster for two-handed weapon damage.
🍯 Jar Shard: Obtained later in the run to increase the damage dealt by the weapon skill.
✨ Spells
☀️ Golden Vow: An incantation that increases overall damage output and defensive negation.
👑 Howl of Shabriri: Provides a massive percentage boost to all damage dealt at the cost of taking increased damage.
🧪 Buffs
🥛 Flask of Wondrous Physick: Combines the Faith-knot Crystal Tear to boost Faith by ten levels for spell requirements and the Spiked Cracked Tear to enhance charged attack power.
🩸 Low-Health Buffer: Intentionally staying at low health triggers the Red-Feathered Branchsword for a continuous passive damage multiplier.
🎯 Tactics
💨 Invincibility Frame Counters: Trigger the Dynast's Finesse weapon skill to dodge backwards through attacks using invincibility frames, then follow up with a heavy attack to perform a lunging thrust.
🔁 Extended Combos: Press the heavy attack button a second time after the initial counter-thrust to execute an extra piercing blow.
🔨 Stance Breaking: Combine fully charged heavy thrusts with the Axe Talisman and Spiked Cracked Tear to break enemy posture quickly.
🩸 Bleed Exploitation: Continuous aggressive poking takes advantage of the high Arcane scaling to trigger rapid blood loss damage on bosses.
📐 NPC Tracking: When fighting non-player character enemies, aim attacks upward to strike them if they attempt to jump over your strikes.
