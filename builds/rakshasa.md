* **Stat Scaling Priorities:** Prioritise Dexterity to its soft cap of 60 first [[02:04](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRCxR4mEAA0&t=124), [13:17](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRCxR4mEAA0&t=797)]. If extra level capacity exists, scale Strength up to 60 for up to a 10% Attack Rating increase [[02:09](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRCxR4mEAA0&t=129)].
* **Poise Breakthrough Threshold:** The secondary survivability gear choice aims precisely at reaching or exceeding the 101 poise value to secure uninterrupted hit trading [[07:41](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRCxR4mEAA0&t=461), [11:13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRCxR4mEAA0&t=673)].
* **Mechanical Drawback:** The weapon applies a passive -10% physical damage negation penalty to the user, which requires mitigation through defensive items and tears [[03:03](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tRCxR4mEAA0&t=183)].

Please ensure you verify this configuration prior to committing your larval tears or spending smithing upgrade materials. 🐛
