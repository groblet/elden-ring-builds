## 📝 Notes
* **Stat Priorities:** Hybrid Strength and Intelligence scaling to maximise both physical and magic damage for the Royal Greatsword.
* **Two-Handing:** Weapon is primarily held two-handed to benefit from the Two-Handed Sword Talisman.
* **Combat Mechanics:** Utilises the crouch poke and overhead heavy attacks to force opponent panic rolls, creating opportunities to land the Wolf's Assault skill combo.
* **Damage Stacking:** The full Rakshasa armour set provides a flat 8% damage increase across the board, pushing the total weapon attack power up to 1329 when fully buffed.

Verify the build before spending larval tears or upgrade materials. 🐛
