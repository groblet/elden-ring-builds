# 🧙 Build Overview
This lightning-themed faith build centres on utilising the **Gravel Stone Seal** for damage-boosting lightning incantations, supplemented by the **Cipher Pata** and **Coded Sword** as lightweight, high-stance-damage melee options. It relies on a high-faith investment to maximise spell output while leveraging specific items to counter lightning-resistant enemies and bosses.

# 📊 Stats
- **Vigor:** 60 (Goal)
- **Mind:** High (to support spell spam)
- **Endurance:** Moderate
- **Strength:** Base requirement (varies)
- **Dexterity:** Base requirement (varies)
- **Intelligence:** Base
- **Faith:** 69+ (for maximum seal scaling)
- **Arcane:** Base (unless using specific items)
- **Total Level:** Aiming for 150+

# ⚔️ Weapons
- **Gravel Stone Seal:** Equipped in the off-hand to gain the passive damage bonus for lightning incantations.
- **Cipher Pata:** Primary melee secondary for pure faith scaling and stance breaking.
- **Coded Sword:** Alternative melee secondary offering excellent range and stance-breaking capability without physical weight requirements.

# 🛡️ Gear
- **Talismans:** Godfrey Icon (for charged spells), Crimson Seed Talisman (for healing efficacy), Dragoncrest Talismans (for survivability), and shard items as required for specific encounters.
- **Flasks:** Prioritise a mix of HP and FP flasks.
- **Consumables:** Greases (e.g., bleed grease for specific bosses), Festering Bloody Finger (for early quest routing).

# ✨ Spells
- **Knight's Lightning Spear:** High-damage lightning projectile.
- **Ancient Dragons' Lightning Strike:** Massive AoE and single-target damage, especially against large bosses.
- **Fortissax's Lightning Spear:** Effective against smaller enemies or for heavy hitting.
- **Pest Threads / Pest Thread Spears:** Essential for shredding large bosses resistant to lightning.
- **Golden Vow:** Crucial damage and defensive buff.
- **Flame, Grant Me Strength:** Provides a significant boost to physical and fire damage.

# 🧪 Buffs
- **Howl of Shabriri:** Significant damage buff at the cost of increased damage taken.
- **Lightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear:** Boosts lightning damage.
- **Golden Vow:** Standard faith-based damage and defense increase.

# 🎯 Tactics
- **Stance Breaking:** Use Cipher Pata or Coded Sword charged attacks to break enemy stances, then switch to heavy-hitting lightning spells like Ancient Dragons' Lightning Strike for maximum punishment.
- **Damage Coverage:** Use lightning incantations as your primary tool, switching to holy-based weapons (Cipher Pata/Coded Sword) for general combat and Pest Threads when encountering bosses resistant to lightning.
- **Positioning:** Maintain distance for spell casting, using the inherent hyper-armour of larger lightning spells to trade hits if necessary, while using lighter melee options for close-quarters stance breaks.
- **Boss Prep:** Apply buffs (Howl of Shabriri, Golden Vow) before entering boss arenas. Utilise the environment (standing in water) to amplify lightning damage when possible.
