## 🧙 Build Overview
- ⚔️ This build focuses on maximising the split-damage potential of the Sword of Night and Flame, allowing the player to melt bosses using high-damage sorcery and incantation stance attacks. The playstyle revolves around a hyper-offensive, low-health setup that rewards careful positioning and boss weakness exploitation.

## 📊 Stats
- 🌟 **Total Level**: High-level endgame setup, optimised for deep attribute allocation.
- 🩸 **Vigor**: Invested sufficiently to survive mandatory environmental damage.
- 🧠 **Mind**: Heavily prioritised to facilitate continuous usage of costly stance skills.
- 🏃 **Endurance**: Maintained at base levels to keep equipment load light.
- 💪 **Strength**: 12 (Minimum attribute requirement to wield the primary weapon).
- 🎯 **Dexterity**: 12 (Minimum attribute requirement to wield the primary weapon).
- 🔮 **Intelligence**: 24 minimum, upgraded heavily to maximise magic laser damage.
- 🙏 **Faith**: 24 minimum, upgraded as a secondary source for fire wave scaling.
- 🧪 **Arcane**: Left at baseline character stats.

## ⚔️ Weapons
- 🗡️ **Sword of Night and Flame +10**: The centrepiece of the build, offering incredible magic and fire skills via its unique stance.
- 📜 **Finger Seal**: Equipped in the off-hand purely to cast foundational damage buffs.
- 🦅 **Red-Feathered Branchsword**: Talisman that increases overall attack power by twenty per cent when health drops below twenty per cent.
- 🦂 **Magic Scorpion Charm**: Talisman utilised during magic-focused encounters to increase magic damage by twelve per cent.
- 🦂 **Fire Scorpion Charm**: Talisman utilised during fire-focused encounters to increase fire damage by twelve per cent.
- 👑 **Rellana's Cameo**: Talisman that enhances the damage of unique stance attacks when the stance is held for a short duration.

## 🛡️ Gear
- 👒 **Alberich's Robe (Altered)**: Lightweight torso piece chosen to ensure fast rolling capabilities.
- 🩳 **Sorcerer Trousers**: Minimalist leg gear used to keep overall equipment weight low.
- 👑 **Godrick's Great Rune**: Activated to grant a flat bonus of five levels to every single attribute.

## ✨ Spells
- 🙏 **Golden Vow**: Incantation used to increase overall damage output and defence.
- 😡 **Howl of Shabriri**: Incantation used to greatly boost attack power at the cost of increased damage taken.

## 🧪 Buffs
- 🧪 **Flask of Wondrous Physick**: Combines the Magic-Shrouding Cracked Tear and Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear to boost both elemental damage paths.
- 💤 **Sleep Pots**: Consumable items deployed to pacify multi-target encounters such as the Godskin Duo.
- 🩸 **Bloody Slash**: Ash of war equipped on a secondary weapon, used solely to drain health safely before a boss fight to activate low-health buffs.

## 🎯 Tactics
- 🔀 **Stance Versatility**: Switch between the two stance variants depending on the target. The light attack unleashes a long-range magic comet that deals heavy stance damage, while the heavy attack releases a wide fire wave for area-of-effect damage.
- 🩸 **High-Risk Setup**: Prior to entering a boss arena, use the secondary weapon skill to intentionally reduce your health below twenty per cent, triggering a massive damage multiplication via your talismans.
- 🎯 **Target Weaknesses**: Match the elemental attack to the specific enemy. Deploy the fire wave against wood-based enemies and Radagon, while prioritising the magic laser against fire-resistant targets like Mohg and Maliketh.
- ⏱️ **Timing and Positioning**: Stance attacks have long execution windows that leave you vulnerable. Position yourself safely behind the boss during their recovery animations to allow the stance talisman to fully charge before firing.
