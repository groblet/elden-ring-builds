### 🎮 Playstyle Summary
Two-hand your primary Greatsword while keeping Freya's Greatsword on your back to receive a passive damage boost to all Redmane skills. Use crouch pokes to pressure enemies and force them to roll. Follow up immediately with Flame of the Redmanes to catch their roll or zone multiple targets with its wide area of effect. Swap your right-hand weapon to mix up standard Lion's Claw and Savage Lion's Claw attacks, exploiting hyper armour to trade damage and pancake opponents.
