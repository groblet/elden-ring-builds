🎯 Tactics
🔄 Heavy-to-Light Combo: Initiate combat encounters by letting the opponent attack, then execute a heavy attack followed immediately by a light attack to deliver an inescapable burst combination.
🛡️ Calculated Trading: Leverage the pierce damage type of the heavy attack to exploit the game's counterattack mechanics, dealing amplified damage when hitting enemies mid-animation.
💥 One-Shot Setup: Activate Royal Knight's Resolve right before engaging in a trade to increase the odds of eliminating opponents in a single sequence.
🧪 Buff Management: Consume Exalted Flesh and Uplifting Aromatic concurrently to raise the weapon's damage rating from 777 to 1,026 before entering engagements.
