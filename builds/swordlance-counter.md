## 🎯 Tactics
 * 🔄 **Heavy-to-Light Combo**: Land a heavy attack during an enemy strike, then follow up immediately with a light attack.
 * 🛡️ **Counter Trading**: Use the pierce damage property of the weapon to exploit counterattack windows.
 * 💥 **Burst Setup**: Apply Royal Knight's Resolve before trading to maximise the chance of a quick elimination.
 * 🧪 **Pre-buffing**: Combine Exalted Flesh and Uplifting Aromatic to raise the weapon attack rating to 1,026.
