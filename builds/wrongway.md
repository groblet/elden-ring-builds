## 🧙 Build Overview

This setup is a high-damage caster build focused on trivialising Elden Ring bosses by melting them from a distance using obnoxiously powerful magic. The playstyle transitions from an early-game gravity sorcerer into a late-game lightning-wielding incantation build that achieves instant phase transitions through massive burst damage and heavy stance breaks.

## ⚔️ Weapons

- ⚡ **Erdtree Seal +10**: The primary catalyst used in the main hand during the late game to achieve optimal incantation scaling for maximum spell damage.
- 🪨 **Gravel Stone Seal**: Kept equipped in the off-hand exclusively to provide a passive fifteen per cent damage boost to all Dragon Cult lightning incantations.
- 🔮 **Lusat's Glintstone Staff +9**: Used during the mid-game sorcery phase to drastically boost spell scaling for high-damage sorceries.
- 🪨 **Meteorite Staff**: The starting early-game staff used to boost gravity sorceries before transitioning to higher-tier setups.
- 👥 **Staff of Loss**: Held in the off-hand during the mid-game to provide a passive ten per cent damage buff to Night Comet.
- 🔥 **Giant's Seal**: Utilised briefly to enhance fire-based incantations against specific targets.
- 🎖️ **Lightning Scorpion Charm**: A key talisman that significantly increases lightning damage output at the cost of slightly reduced damage negation.
- 🎖️ **Flock's Canvas Talisman**: Greatly increases the overall potency of all equipped incantations.
- 🎖️ **Radagon Icon**: Shortens the casting time of all sorceries and incantations to allow faster spell spamming.
- 🎖️ **Godfrey Icon**: Raises the attack power of charged spells and skills, perfectly pairing with heavy lightning strikes.
- 🎖️ **Graven-School Talisman**: Equipped early in the run to provide a straightforward four per cent boost to sorceries.
- 🎖️ **Stargazer Heirloom**: Used temporarily to boost intelligence stats by five points to meet heavy spell requirements.

## 🛡️ Gear

- 👑 **Snow Witch Hat**: Equipped in the head slot primarily for personal aesthetic preference and style.
- 👕 **Death Knight Armor**: The chest piece worn as part of the late-game lightning setup.
- 🧤 **Death Knight Gauntlets**: Equipped to stack passive damage multipliers.
- 🥾 **Death Knight Greaves**: Worn alongside the other set pieces to achieve a combined six per cent damage amplification for Dragon Cult lightning spells.

## ✨ Spells

- ⚡ **Knight's Lightning Spear**: The premier projectile spell from the downloadable content that serves as the primary ranged option for tracking and staggering mobile targets.
- ⛈️ **Ancient Dragon's Lightning Strike**: A devastating area-of-effect incantation that summons multiple lightning strikes to shred massive boss hitboxes.
- ☀️ **Golden Vow**: A fundamental defensive and offensive aura buff that raises overall damage dealt and damage negation.
- 🐺 **Howl of Shabriri**: A high-risk buff that amplifies all damage output by twenty-five per cent whilst increasing incoming damage and enemy aggression.
- 🔥 **Flame, Grant Me Strength**: Used to enhance physical damage for early sorceries and fire damage for specialised boss encounters.
- 🌳 **Blessing of the Erdtree**: Cast to provide continuous health regeneration to counteract self-draining mechanics.
- 🪨 **Rock Sling**: The early-game gravity sorcery relied upon for heavy physical stance damage and reliable tracking.
- ☄️ **Comet**: A reliable, hard-hitting glintstone sorcery used for direct mid-game ranged damage.
- 🌌 **Stars of Ruin**: A tracking sorcery that unleashes a barrage of homing magical projectiles.
- 👁️ **Night Comet**: An invisible sorcery that enemies fail to read or dodge, making it exceptionally effective against nimble opponents.
- 🟥 **Burn, O Flame!**: A fire incantation that unleashes pillars of flame from the ground, used against specific plant-based enemies.
- 🌙 **Giant Golden Arc**: A wide-reaching holy incantation deployed against targets weak to holy elements.

## 🧪 Buffs

- 🧪 **Lightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear**: Mixed into the Wondrous Physick to heavily boost all lightning-based elemental attacks.
- 🧪 **Bloodsucking Cracked Tear**: Mixed into the Wondrous Physick to grant a massive twenty per cent generic damage boost at the cost of draining health over time.
- 🧪 **Cerulean Hidden Tear**: Used early in the journey to eliminate all focus point consumption for a short period.
- 💤 **Sleep Pots**: Crafted consumables used to put aggressive groups of enemies completely to sleep, allowing them to be fought one at a time.
- ⛓️ **Margit's Shackle & Mohg's Shackle**: Utility items used to pin specific bosses to the ground at the start of an encounter for free damage windows.
- 🤱 **Baldachin's Blessing**: An unintended inventory item that passively reduces maximum health throughout the run.

## 🎯 Tactics

- 🔄 **Pre-Fight Preparation**: Before crossing the boss fog, cast Golden Vow, follow up with either Howl of Shabriri or Flame Grant Me Strength, and activate Blessing of the Erdtree to counteract health drain. Conclude by drinking the Flask of Wondrous Physick to activate the elemental and bloodsucking multipliers.
- 👥 **Summon Management**: Summon Black Knife Tiche +10 immediately upon entering the arena. Tiche acts as an excellent distraction, using agile dodging to pull aggression away from the caster whilst applying a maximum health burn with Destined Death.
- 🏹 **Confusing Enemy AI**: Cast Knight's Lightning Spear against highly mobile or evasive bosses like Malenia or hostile non-player characters. The delayed multi-projectiles disrupt their dodge timing, causing them to walk directly into the damage.
- 💥 **Melting Massive Targets**: For massive opponents such as Dragonlord Placidusax, Fire Giant, or Rykard, position directly underneath or adjacent to their hitbox and unleash a fully charged Ancient Dragon's Lightning Strike. This forces almost every single bolt to connect simultaneously, deleting entire health bars instantly.
- 💤 **Crowd Control**: Utilise Sleep Pots against multi-boss encounters like the Godskin Duo to completely lock down one target while focusing down the other without disruption.
