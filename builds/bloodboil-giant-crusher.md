🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build focuses on pure strength and hyper-offensive trading. Start by consuming the Bloodboil Aromatic to massively boost your attack rating. Utilize the Uplifting Aromatic and weapon greases for additional damage. The core of the combat strategy relies on the crouch attack, which benefits from the Talisman setup and specific armour pieces to deal immense damage, frequently two-shotting opponents. Maintain your health with regeneration incantations and manage your poise breakpoints to effectively trade hits.
