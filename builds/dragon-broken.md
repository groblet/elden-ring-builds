## 🗡️ Armaments
* **Dragon Communion Seal +10** (Dual-wielded in New Game Plus for a stacking 15% damage bonus per seal to Dragon Communion incantations).
* **Highland Axe w/ Poison** increases the damage of Roars by 10%.

## 🛡️ Armour
* **No Armour:** You must wear completely empty armour slots to use the Rock Heart transformation item.

## 💍 Talismans
* **Blue Dancer Charm:** Provides a 15% damage bonus to all physical damage (including Dragonmaw, Dragonclaw, Greyoll's Roar, rot breaths, and a 6% bonus to Bayle's Flame Lightning) because you are at the lowest equipment load.
* **Flock's Canvas Talisman:** Enhances all incantation damage regardless of the element.
* **Faithful's Canvas Talisman:** Enhances all incantation damage regardless of the element.
* **Kindred of Rot:** Raises attack power by 20% for 20 seconds after  Poisoning or  Rot.
* **Situational Fourth Slot:** 
  * **Roar Medallion:** Buffs all breath attacks, Bayle's Tyranny, and Greyoll's Roar.
  * **Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman:** Boosts damage negation to mitigate the low defences of having no armour.
  * **Dread Talisman / Fire Scorpion Charm:** Mentioned as optional additions specifically to maximize fire damage setups.

## ✨ Sorceries & Incantations
* **Golden Vow:** Aura buff providing 15% extra damage.
* **Howl of Shabiri:** Body buff providing 25% extra damage (can be replaced by Flame, Grant Me Strength or Black Flame's Protection for better survivability).
* **Bayle's Flame Lightning:** Primary offensive option that launches you forward, dealing physical, lightning, and fire damage with high hyper armour. Allows immediate follow-up casting.
* **Dragonmaw:** Extremely hard-hitting physical bite with infinite hyper armour.
* **Bayle's Tyranny:** Deals massive lightning and fire magma damage. Best used as a close-range finisher on stance-broken bosses or to clear packed groups of enemies.
* **Dragonclaw:** High-efficiency melee attack with great area-of-effect and hyper armour. Useful for open-world exploration.
* **Theodorix's Magma:** Named dragon breath that can be cast twice sequentially on horseback.
* **Ghostflame Breath:** Deals magic damage and inflicts frostbite. Consolidates the roles of Borealis's Mist and Smarag's Glintstone Breath into a single spell slot.
* **Greyoll's Roar:** Debuff and physical damage spell that benefits from the Roar Medallion.

## 🧴 Buffs & Consumables
1. **Rock Heart:** Transformation item used while unarmoured to enter dragon form. Grants a flat 10% damage negation, 50 to all status resistances, and a unique 20% damage buff to Dragon Communion incantations.
2. **Flask of Wondrous Physick:** Contains the Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear (Flame Tear) to boost fire-based incantations.
3. **Golden Vow**
4. **Howl of Shabiri** (or alternative body buff)

## 📝 Notes
* **Stat Priorities:** Target 60 Vigour due to your fragile unarmoured state. Mind should be at least 38 to support high FP costs, but can be raised to 50 or 60 later. Faith soft caps at 45 for the Dragon Communion Seal. Arcane is your primary damage scaling stat which soft caps at 45, but you must reach at least 53 Arcane to meet the minimum requirements for all spells. Endurance is low priority as equipment load is irrelevant without armour.
* **Spell Ordering:** Order your memory slots so Dragonmaw sits directly after Bayle's Flame Lightning. This allows you to cycle straight into Dragonmaw's infinite hyper armour if you need to trade hits after a leap. Place Bayle's Tyranny immediately after Dragonmaw to access it quickly during stance breaks.

Verify the build details before spending larval tears or upgrade materials. 🐛
