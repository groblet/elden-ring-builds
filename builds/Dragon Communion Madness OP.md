## 🎮 Playstyle Summary
This build relies entirely on high-damage Dragon Communion incantations cast through the Erdtree Seal in the main hand whilst holding the Dragon Communion Seal in the off-hand to benefit from its passive scaling buffs.

The playstyle switches between pure physical setups using Dragonmouth and Dragonclaw, which are heavily boosted by the Blue Dancer Charm due to the zero-weight Dragon Form provided by the Rock Heart, and massive elemental breaths tailored to boss weaknesses.

Initiate combat by consuming the Rock Heart to enter dragon form and activate its one-minute twenty per cent damage buff. For mobile or aggressive bosses, rely on the hyper armour of Dragonclaw and the high stance damage of Dragonmouth to create openings. 

For large or slow bosses, apply Greyoll's Roar to debuff their defence and attack, then use the appropriate elemental breath like Borealis's Mist or Agheel's Flame to melt their health bars from mid-range.

Self-inflict madness briefly using the Madding Hand before a fight to trigger the Aged One's Exaltation for an additional damage spike. Manage FP carefully, as these high-cost spells leave minimal room for missed casts.
