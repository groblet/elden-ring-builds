## 🧙 Build Overview
 * 🐍 **Theme and Playstyle**: A Strength and Arcane build centred on a colossal Greatsword paired with poison mechanics to maximise physical damage and blood loss accumulation during PvP invasions.
## 📊 Stats
 * 📈 **Total Level**: Meta invasion level (typically Rune Level 125 or 150).
 * ❤️ **Vigor**: High priority (typically 60 for PvP survivability).
 * 🧪 **Mind**: Kept at base level.
 * ⚡ **Endurance**: Maintained high enough to manage heavy equip load alongside Great-Jar's Arsenal.
 * 💪 **Strength**: Primary scaling attribute to maximise Greatsword physical damage.
 * 🎯 **Dexterity**: Kept at minimum weapon requirements.
 * 🧠 **Intelligence**: Base level.
 * 🙏 **Faith**: Base level.
 * 🧬 **Arcane**: Heavily invested to scale poison utility and accelerate blood loss buildup.
## ⚔️ Weapons
 * 🗡️ **Greatsword**: Main two-handed weapon, equipped with the Poison Mist Ash of War for high raw damage and blood loss buildup.
 * 🤚 **Poisoned Hand**: Off-hand weapon kept in the setup to passively trigger a damage buff when poison is nearby.
 * 💍 **Kindred of Rot's Exaltation**: Talisman that increases attack power when poisoning occurs in the vicinity.
 * 💍 **Shard of Alexander**: Talisman that boosts the skill damage of the Poison Mist Ash of War.
 * 💍 **Two-Handed Sword Talisman**: Talisman providing a 10% damage increase when two-handing the Greatsword.
 * 💍 **Great-Jar's Arsenal**: Talisman required to handle the high equipment weight of the build.
 * 💍 **Crimson Seed Talisman**: Swapped in contextually to increase health recovery from flasks.
## 🛡️ Gear
 * 🍄 **Mushroom Crown**: Mandatory headpiece that boosts attack power whenever poison is triggered nearby.
 * 🛡️ **Armour Pieces**: Remaining slots are flexible, chosen to optimise poise and physical defence.
## 🧪 Buffs
 * 🧪 **Blood Boil Aromatic**: Consumable that severely increases physical damage at the cost of reduced defences.
 * 💨 **Poison Mist**: Ash of War that applies a poison coating to the weapon and creates a local toxic cloud.
 * 💦 **Deadly Poison Spray**: Ash of War used on a secondary tool to easily self-poison, instantly triggering all poison-related damage multipliers.
 * 🛡️ **Uplifting Aromatic**: Consumable used to gain a temporary protective bubble shield during buffing routines.
 * 🦀 **Boiled Crab**: Consumable used to mitigate physical damage.
## 🎯 Tactics
 * 🔄 **The Roll-Catch Combo**: Deliver a crouch-poke with the Greatsword, then follow up immediately with the Poison Mist Ash of War to catch opponents who panic-roll from the initial stagger.
 * 📈 **Damage Stacking**: Consume the Blood Boil Aromatic first. Reapply poison by either self-poisoning or clouding an enemy to trigger the passive attack multipliers from the Mushroom Crown, Kindred of Rot's Exaltation, and Poisoned Hand simultaneously.
 * 👥 **Crowd Management**: Use the wide, sweeping pattern of the Greatsword's R2 heavy attacks to zone out multiple opponents and deal with aggressive groups.
 * 🛡️ **Hyper-Armour Trading**: Leverage the high poise and hyper-armour of the colossal Greatsword to trade hits with enemies, using the massive fully-buffed attack power to eliminate players quickly.
