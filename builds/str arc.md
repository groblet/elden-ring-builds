This guide outlines the most effective Strength Arcane hybrid build for level 150 PvP.

## 📊 Attributes

- Vigor: 60
- Mind: Base
- Endurance: 28 to 30 (adjust to equip load)
- Strength: 54
- Dexterity: 16 to 18 (meeting weapon requirements)
- Intelligence: Base
- Faith: Base
- Arcane: 45

## 🗡️ Equipment

- Right Hand: Ancient Meteoric Greatsword, Bloodfiend's Sacred Spear, or your preferred strength-scaling weapon.
- Left Hand: Secondary weapon or defensive tool for utility.
- Armour: Aim for 88 passive poise. Utilize armour combinations such as the Albinuric mask, Verdigris armour, Veteran's gauntlets, and Verdigris legs to balance weight and poise.

## 💍 Talismans

- Bull-Goat's Talisman
- Great-Jar's Arsenal
- Two-Handed Sword Talisman
- Flex slot (e.g., Blue Feathered Branchsword)

## ✨ Spells & Ash of War

- Sorceries/Incantations: Not the primary focus of this variant, but keep utility options available.
- Ash of War: Apply Seppuku to boost damage and bleed buildup.

## 🎮 Playstyle Summary

- Prioritise 60 Vigor and 45 Arcane to hit crucial health and status buildup breakpoints.
- Two-hand your weapons to gain a 50% artificial strength bonus, allowing you to hit the 54 Strength soft cap effectively for 80 Strength scaling.
- Focus on versatility and adapting your weapon choice based on the situation. Use the Ancient Meteoric Greatsword for its high damage and great Ash of War, or the Bloodfiend's Sacred Spear for powerful bleed procs.
- Manage your inventory to stay at or above 88 passive poise to prevent stun from common weapon types.
- Use Seppuku to trigger damage-boosting buffs like Lord of Blood's Exultation.
