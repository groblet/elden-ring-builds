## 🎮 Playstyle Summary

This build focuses on inducing madness to enable high damage output and crowd control. Use the Madding Hand weapon skill to apply madness build-up on enemies. While the target is trapped in the animation, quickly transition into high-damage area of effect incantations like Wrath of Gold or Roar of Rugalea. The Aged One's Exaltation boosts attack power when madness is in the vicinity, creating a synergistic loop with the weapon skill. Success requires isolating individual targets, as long combo animations leave you vulnerable to interruption from other opponents. Prioritise maintaining distance or using terrain to separate foes before initiating your combo chain.
