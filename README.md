## 🛡️ Collection 2: Elden Ring PvE Inventory Guide

These optimized layouts are categorized by dominant offensive stats and updated for Shadow of the Erdtree.

### Prefatory Rules & Notes

- **DLC Readiness**: Features new equipment and upgrades from Shadow of the Erdtree.
- **Stats Maintained**: 60 VIG / 51 POISE / 16 END on all setups for optimized weight-to-negation ratios.
- **Level 125 Choice**: Consciously kept at RL125 to strip down to essentials, leaving personal adaptation room.
- **No Buff Stacking**: Direct, high-impact structures without excessive preparation or talisman swapping.
- **Heavier Equipment Slots**: If a setup warns "Overloaded" or "Heavy Load," the secondary buff weapon is meant to be used for its skill, then unequipped directly after.
- **Armor Optimization**: Maximum negation at minimum weight while strictly hitting the 51 poise breakpoint.
